Arcade Project Summary

The purpose of Galaga is to be able to fight off the enemy bugs from
destroying your starship. With limited amount of bullets and an increasing
rate of enemy bugs appearing, you must keep fighting until you either
run out of bullets or destroy your ship. We focused on using the scripts
that sent messages out to other sprites and the variable "game_over", which
could be triggered in countless ways from losing all the bullets, running
into an enemy, or losing all your lives. These scripts allow the use of
the final sprite to trigger both the notification that the game has to
players and the "stop" script that concludes the game, thus the player has
to click the green flag to restart the game. We encountered glitches from
the Scratch itself with the positions of the axes being offset, finding
scripts that overlapped each other causing multiple triggers, and the
best method to clean the scripts to allow us to see the progress and the
necessary fixes to our game.
